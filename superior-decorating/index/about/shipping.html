Shipping & Returns

Our policies are below:

Shipping Policy

Our goal is to make your shopping experience simple and to ensure your satisfaction. SD provides free shipping for all purchases from our website to 48 States of US only, all free shipping service is ground shipping, which may take 7-10 business days, if you want you item be shipped in express way, State of Hawaii, State of Alaska or outside of US, you can call our customer service at 732-698-9988 or e-mail to customerserivce@superiordecoratingllc.com for a shipping quotation.

Shipping Damage

When you received your product, you should inspect and make sure the delivery is no damage on the package. If the box is damaged, you should open the package and check the item inside, if you found a damaged item, you can sign for the delivery, and write down your note on the receipt to the driver, picture it for a proof of damage if possible, then, you must notify us within 48 hours, we will file a claim from our shipping company, otherwise, any shipping damage will be your responsibility.

Return Policy

Most items may be returned within 14 days of receipt for a refund or exchange. This includes all costs incurred by SD for transporting items promoted with free shipping. Items must be returned at the customer's expense, in new condition, in the original box with the manufacturer's packaging and information. No returns for refunds or exchanges will be granted after 14 days of receipt or item was being installed. A Return Merchandise Authorization (RMA) number is required on all returns. Please contact our Customer Service at (732) 698-9988 or customerservice@superiordecoratingllc.com to obtain a Return Merchandise Authorization (RMA) number.

Return Credit

Your credit will be returned to your original credit card in 9-14 business days from the time SD receives your returned item.

Defective item

If the product you received has manufacturing defects; contact us within 48 hours of receiving the product and SD will replace the item or parts. We will issue an RMA and give you instructions to ship back the products.

Pricing

Due to increasing costs of brass and raw materials, prices are subject to change without notice. SD assumes no responsibility for typographical errors.
